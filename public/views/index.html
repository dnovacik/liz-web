liz Features

Masternodes are used to confirm transactions as fast as the network can transport them, once confirmed they are unchangeable and secured in the network.

Speed

Transactions can be confirmed in fractions of a second.

Zero Fees

Transaction fees are annoying, so having none was an imporant key requirement during the design.

Scalable

Scalability is one of the major problems in crypto currencies right now. Our system has a high transaction rate that can scale up with the number of masternodes.

Masternodes

The processing of transactions relies on masternodes, which provide a steady income to their operators.

Self funded

There are no investors, and no big money involved. Everything is being developed by dedicated members of the liz community.

Privacy

We have a strong privacy feature on our roadmap.
